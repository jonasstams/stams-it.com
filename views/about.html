A few words about me

Hey people! First of all thank you for visiting my site and I hope that it can give you a little view into my life.

My name is Jonas Stams and I live in a small, quiet town that's called Ophoven. It's near Maaseik, a city that most people will know. I'm already busy with computers for more than 14 years, and so I discovered my talent. It feels like I can understand what a computer means. In 2011 I went to a new secondary school where you could choose for IT-management. It was 11 hours of IT a week, so this was something for me. Here I started to learn a lot of new things! And now, 5 years later, I still love to work on a PC. I've learned a lot and I'm still studying IT. Now at PXL a high school in Hasselt. I found out that I'm a quick learner, not in every class, but the things I really want to do in the future, stuck in my head so easily. I love programming and just love to work with code. On the other side, I have a head full of ideas. I keep on thinking how I can make success in life. Of course I first need to work to get some good experience, but my dream is to start something myself. I believe in myself and I will do everything to become one of the best in the things that I do. First of all I want to help my parents, so they don't have to struggle anymore at the end of their life. They gave me everything, and they will get it all back for sure!
