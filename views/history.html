My History

On one creative day, I felt like writing a fairy-tale about my life.

A long time ago, in a village far, far away, there was a young boy named Jonas... He had really good parents and one big brother. Together they lived in a nice house with a big garden. One day, the parents of Jonas bought a computer because Jonas really wanted a computer! They had no internet connection so the only thing Jonas could do was drawing stuff in Paint. Jonas liked to tell his mom that he was surfing on the internet, he just opened Notepad and started typing random things. His mom always answered on a funny way: "Don't you dare!". Jonas kept doing this because he liked her reaction.

A couple of years later, they got internet connection and Jonas liked to play games on Minigames.com. One day the uncle of Jonas came to their house, he came because Jonas asked him to put Windows XP on the computer that was still running on Windows 98. Jonas wanted to see how this worked and was watching the screen the whole day. He was so happy with the new Windows XP because it looked so fancy! This old pc was getting slow and Jonas really wanted a new computer. He asked his parents and they told him that he had to get a good score on school. So he really did his best because he wanted the computer. [More coming soon]
